THE GENESIS

1970: The Birth

Black Sabbath's self-titled debut changed everything. The heavy riffs and dark themes set the blueprint for a whole new genre.

Fun Fact: Tony Iommi’s missing fingertips actually contributed to the "heavy" sound because he had to downtune his guitar strings.

1980s: The Expansion

The New Wave of British Heavy Metal and the rise of Thrash in the US pushed metal into the mainstream consciousness.
